# <a name="revenue-recognition-reallocation"></a><span data-ttu-id="9330b-104">Neuzuweisung der Umsatzerkennung</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9330b-104">Revenue recognition reallocation</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]
