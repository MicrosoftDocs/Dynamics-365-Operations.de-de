[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="b47bb-104">Sie können Zeitfenster verwenden, um die Planung von Serviceauftragspositionen zu optimieren.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b47bb-104">You can use time windows to optimize the scheduling of service order lines.</span></span>
